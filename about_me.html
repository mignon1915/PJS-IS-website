A Little Bit About Me

Information Science

I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Information Science at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Additionally, I serve as the vice president of the UNC chapter of asis&t, a professional society for information science which fosters intellectual discourse and career development for information professionals worldwide.

As a person who finds organization of information fascinating, I enjoy the challenge of discovering the most efficient and orderly way of completing a task. I love thinking about how to make information more accessible and navigable for people.

Music

I am a professional singer and voice teacher, specifically in the fields of opera, oratorio, and concert work. I received my Bachelor of Music in Performance degree from the University of North Carolina at Greensboro in 2006. I received my Master of Music in Performance degree from the University of Michigan in 2009.
